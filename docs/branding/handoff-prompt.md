# ProtocolLab Brand Production Handoff Prompt

Copy the block below into a design task. Attach or link the Incursa UI Kit
brand exports, the current ProtocolLab public-site screenshots, and the
SpecTrace brand package as visual references.

---

Create a complete product identity for **ProtocolLab**, an Incursa public
technical project.

ProtocolLab provides implementation-neutral public contracts for protocol
measurement evidence. It defines shared documents for describing what was
selected, what was measured, and what a result may claim. Its value is honest,
inspectable evidence: explicit provenance, warnings, unsupported states, and
comparability boundaries. It is not a certification authority, official
benchmark arbiter, or guarantee of production readiness.

The audience is protocol implementers, performance/interoperability engineers,
lab operators, evidence reviewers, and contributors consuming schemas,
scenarios, packages, and report contracts.

Use this brand personality: precise, technical, calm, transparent,
operational, and evidence-first. Avoid flashy futurism, speed/racing language,
and generic developer-tool imagery.

Use `ProtocolLab` as the formal product name and wordmark. The primary lockup
is the product mark plus `ProtocolLab`. Create a subordinate endorsed lockup
with `BY INCURSA`. Do not use the Incursa `n` arch as the product icon.

Develop the **Measurement Gate** concept. Build a roughly square, geometric
mark from two parallel protocol lanes passing through a central calibration
gate or aperture. Simple packet forms or registration ticks may clarify the
measurement idea. It must communicate that traffic passes through a declared
measurement boundary and produces reviewable evidence. The mark must work in
one color and remain recognizable at 16, 24, and 32 pixels.

Do not use or resemble:

- the Incursa `n` arch;
- the SpecTrace E3 bracket/diamond/trace-to-tile mark;
- a flask, beaker, atom, globe, generic connected-node diagram, shield,
  checkmark, seal, speedometer, rocket, trophy, or protocol-specific wire icon.

Use this palette:

- Protocol Teal `#0F766E`;
- Deep Teal `#115E59`;
- Lab Ink `#0F172A`;
- Signal Cyan `#0F8BA0` for diagrams/large accents only;
- Measurement Mist `#E0F4F1`;
- White `#FFFFFF`;
- Incursa Indigo `#2B2E69` only for the Incursa endorsement.

Use IBM Plex Sans SemiBold/Bold as the wordmark starting point and IBM Plex
Mono Medium for technical eyebrow labels. Keep the name immediately readable.
Convert lettering to outlines in public SVG exports, retain a private editable
master with live type, and do not bundle font software into the public assets.

Use these approved messages:

- descriptor: `Public contracts for protocol measurement evidence.`
- short line: `Shared contracts. Inspectable evidence.`
- social headline: `Protocol measurement with claims tied to evidence.`
- social eyebrow: `CONTRACT -> RUN -> EVIDENCE`
- attribution: `PROTOCOLLAB / BY INCURSA`

Do not use `certified`, `official benchmark`, `verified performance`,
`industry standard`, `production ready`, or `fair comparison` as claims.

Work in three checkpoints:

1. Show up to three black-and-white variations within the Measurement Gate
   direction. Include 16/24/32-pixel tests and recommend one route.
2. Develop the approved/recommended route into color, white, mono, horizontal,
   and Incursa-endorsed lockups. Show light/dark and avatar-crop proofs.
3. Produce the full export family specified below. If review is not available,
   complete only the recommended route and keep alternatives as sketches.

Required final assets:

- standalone mark: color, white, and mono SVG;
- horizontal logo: color and white SVG;
- Incursa-endorsed lockup: color and white SVG;
- transparent README headers: color and white, `430 x 160` viewBox;
- GitHub avatar PNG, `512 x 512`;
- GitHub social preview PNG, `1280 x 640`, under 1 MB;
- Open Graph social card PNG, `1200 x 630`;
- adaptive, light, and dark SVG favicons;
- multi-frame ICO at 16/32/48 pixels;
- PNG icons at 16, 32, 192, and 512 pixels;
- opaque Apple touch icon at 180 pixels;
- concept, small-size, crop, light/dark, grayscale, and social-preview proofs;
- palette JSON, asset inventory CSV, font/license notes, and SHA-256 hashes.

For the social images, place the product and headline on the left and a sparse,
abstract Measurement Gate/protocol-lane composition on the right. Use a solid
Lab Ink background, keep essential content inside a 7 percent safe margin, and
do not invent data values or charts.

Public SVGs must have a valid viewBox, explicit colors, useful title/description
metadata, no external dependencies, no scripts, no embedded raster images, no
font files, and no unnecessary editor metadata. Make public wordmarks
self-contained outlines. Provide an optically corrected simplified favicon if
the full mark does not survive at 16 pixels.

Also provide usage notes covering clear space, minimum sizes, geometry,
approved backgrounds, color variants, incorrect use, alt-text guidance, and
which source files remain private rather than being committed publicly.

The final identity must feel related to Incursa through typography, restraint,
and production quality while being unmistakably different from both the
Incursa company mark and SpecTrace.
