# Incursa Contributor Agreement v1

This repository requires a contributor agreement for all non-allowlisted code,
documentation, test, build, and workflow contributions.

By contributing to Protocol Lab, you confirm that:

- you have the right to submit the contribution;
- you are not knowingly submitting third-party material without permission;
- you grant Incursa the rights needed to use, reproduce, modify, distribute,
  and sublicense the contribution as part of Protocol Lab and related project
  material;
- your signed pull request comment may be recorded by the automated
  contributor agreement gate for compliance and auditability.

To sign this agreement on a pull request, comment exactly:

> I have read the Incursa Contributor Agreement and I hereby assign my
> contribution rights as described.

The required merge gate for this repository is the `Contributor Agreement`
status check. A pull request cannot be merged until the check passes or the
contributor is allowlisted by the workflow.
