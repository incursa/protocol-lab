# ProtocolLab Brand Acceptance Checklist

Use this checklist before accepting a concept, merging exports, replacing site
assets, or uploading a GitHub social preview.

## Brand And Message

- [ ] The product name is `ProtocolLab` in the mark and formal lockups.
- [ ] The product is not described as a certification authority, official
      benchmark, universal performance ranking, or production-readiness proof.
- [ ] The identity feels precise, operational, calm, and evidence-oriented.
- [ ] The social copy matches the approved message spine.
- [ ] `BY INCURSA` is subordinate and does not compete with the product name.

## Distinction

- [ ] The mark cannot be mistaken for the Incursa `n`.
- [ ] The mark cannot be mistaken for the SpecTrace E3 symbol or its
      bracket/diamond/trace-to-tile construction.
- [ ] The mark does not use a checkmark, seal, shield, or trophy that implies
      certification.
- [ ] The mark does not use a flask, atom, generic network nodes, rocket,
      speedometer, or protocol-specific symbol.
- [ ] The one-color silhouette is recognizable without explanatory text.

## Small-Size And Crop Proof

- [ ] The favicon is legible at 16 pixels at 100 percent zoom.
- [ ] The standard mark is legible at 24 and 32 pixels.
- [ ] The horizontal lockup is readable at 140 pixels wide.
- [ ] The avatar survives square, rounded-square, and circular crops.
- [ ] The README header works in GitHub light and dark appearance.
- [ ] No essential stroke closes, disappears, or becomes uneven after raster
      export.

## Color And Accessibility

- [ ] Color, white, and mono variants are present.
- [ ] The color mark works on white and Measurement Mist.
- [ ] The white mark works on Lab Ink and Deep Teal.
- [ ] Adjacent text meets the appropriate WCAG contrast requirement.
- [ ] Signal Cyan is not used for small text on white.
- [ ] Grayscale and common color-vision-deficiency proofs remain intelligible.
- [ ] Usage notes explain meaningful alt text versus decorative empty alt text.

## Asset Completeness

- [ ] Every required filename in
      [`asset-production-spec.md`](asset-production-spec.md) is present.
- [ ] Raster dimensions match the manifest exactly and no raster was upscaled.
- [ ] The ICO contains 16, 32, and 48 pixel frames.
- [ ] The GitHub preview is `1280 x 640` and under 1 MB.
- [ ] The Open Graph card is `1200 x 630`.
- [ ] The inventory records dimensions/viewBox, variant, purpose, source
      version, and SHA-256 hash.

## SVG And Source Quality

- [ ] Every SVG has a correct viewBox and no clipped/off-canvas art.
- [ ] Public SVGs contain no scripts, external dependencies, embedded raster
      images, font files, or live wordmark text.
- [ ] Standalone SVGs have unique title and description metadata.
- [ ] White variants contain literal visible white geometry.
- [ ] Editor metadata, hidden layers, and unused definitions are removed.
- [ ] A private editable master with live type and an outlined public master are
      both delivered to the owner.
- [ ] Font names, sources, and license notes are recorded.

## Social And Public-Surface Proof

- [ ] Social-card text remains inside the 7 percent safe area.
- [ ] The social artwork contains no invented measurements or misleading chart
      values.
- [ ] The mark is tested in the public site's 32-pixel header treatment and
      footer treatment.
- [ ] The endorsed lockup is tested on an Incursa corporate surface without
      changing the product name.
- [ ] The proposed mark remains recognizable next to SpecTrace and the Incursa
      corporate mark in a family contact sheet.

## Ownership And Release Gate

- [ ] The owner approved the chosen concept and lockup in writing.
- [ ] The owner recorded the approval date and source-master version.
- [ ] Trademark/conflict review status is recorded; a visual review alone is
      not represented as legal clearance.
- [ ] The brand-asset license boundary is explicitly approved before public
      assets are merged.
- [ ] Any trademark, notice, or brand-license text was reviewed for this
      product rather than copied mechanically from SpecTrace.
- [ ] Private editable masters and font files are excluded from the public
      repository unless separately approved.
- [ ] Site, repository, docs-mirror, and corporate-site integration are tracked
      as separate follow-up changes.
